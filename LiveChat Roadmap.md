**Date:** 2026-01-15
**Goal:** Transform from "Support Tool" to "Sales Engine"

---

## 1. Executive Goal

The current system is a solid communication tool. To maximize ROI, we must shift focus from "Talking" to "Converting". The following roadmap outlines the high-impact features required to achieve this.
